FAQ's

Q1. What type of cuisine does La Meridian offer?

We offer a wide variety of cuisines, including traditional Bangladeshi dishes, continental meals, and fusion food. Our menu is carefully curated to cater to diverse tastes.

Q2. Is there any discount on meals at La Meridian?

Yes, we offer a 10% discount on every meal, applicable to both dine-in and takeout orders. Enjoy delicious meals at an even more affordable price!

Q3.Does La Meridian provide home delivery services?

Yes, we offer home delivery services within Dhaka city. You can place your order online or call us, and we will ensure a prompt delivery to your doorstep

Q4.What are the restaurant’s opening hours?

We are open every day from 12:00 PM to 11:00 PM, including weekends and holidays. Feel free to visit us during these hours to enjoy a delightful meal.

Q5. Can I make a reservation in advance?

Yes, you can make reservations by calling us directly or using our online booking system. We recommend making reservations, especially during weekends and holidays, to avoid long waits.
